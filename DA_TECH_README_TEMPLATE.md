# DA Tech Health AI Platform

**Healthcare-first AI platform for clinical workflow intelligence, AI-assisted execution, model orchestration, governance, and human-in-the-loop decision support.**

---

## Overview

DA Tech is a multi-domain AI platform with Healthcare as the first vertical. The platform is designed to support intelligent execution workflows where clinical inputs, model outputs, evidence, quality gates, monitoring signals, and human review can be tracked as part of a governed AI workflow.

The platform concept moves beyond simple CRUD software toward **AI-assisted execution**: a system where healthcare workflows can be analyzed, supported, monitored, governed, and improved over time.

---

## Problem

Healthcare organizations face increasing pressure from:
- Clinical documentation burden
- Workflow inefficiencies
- Information overload
- Fragmented clinical data
- Limited decision-support integration
- Need for safe AI adoption
- Need for model monitoring, governance, and human oversight

AI systems in healthcare must be clinically useful, explainable, monitored, and governed. They cannot be treated as black-box automation tools.
